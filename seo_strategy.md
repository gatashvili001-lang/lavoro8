# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Public job listing, job detail, and job landing pages
- Blog pages
- Legal and trust pages that are publicly accessible

## Out of scope
- Authenticated profile/dashboard surfaces (`/profilo`, `/profile`)
- Admin pages (`/admin`)
- Clerk authentication flows (`/sign-in`, `/sign-up`)

## Target audience
- Job seekers across Italy and broader Europe, especially blue-collar and service roles looking for cross-border work.
- Employers recruiting for blue-collar and service roles in Italy and other European markets.
